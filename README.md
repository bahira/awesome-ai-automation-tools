## Getting Started with AI Automation

### Step 1: Assess Your Business Needs
1. Identify repetitive tasks (manual data entry, reporting, scheduling)
2. Calculate current time waste (typically 10-15 hours/week)
3. Determine ROI targets (aim for 2x efficiency gain)

### Step 2: Choose Your Automation Approach
- **Quick wins:** Use no-code tools like Zapier or Make
- **Long-term solution:** Build custom Python scripts
- **Hybrid approach:** Combine tools for maximum flexibility

## FAQ

### What's the difference between no-code and custom AI automation?
No-code tools are faster to implement but less flexible. Custom scripts offer unlimited customization but require more technical expertise.

### How much does AI automation cost?
Costs range from $0 (open-source scripts) to $500+ per hour for custom development. Subscription tools typically cost $15-50/user/month.

### Can small businesses benefit from AI automation?
Absolutely! Many tools have affordable pricing tiers specifically designed for small businesses and startups.
